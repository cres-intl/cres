Pathway to Success

A glimpse into our impactful Career Counseling Session

On 19th October 2024, we had the privilege of organizing a Career Counseling Session under the banner of C.R.E.S. International, where around 210 students from schools across Pakistan took part!

A special thank you to our esteemed guest speakers:
- Mohammad Dawar Zahid (Founder of MedQuack, 2nd-year Medical Student at AKU), who shared invaluable insights on getting into medical schools, both in Pakistan and abroad. His guidance on preparing for medical university admissions was truly eye-opening for aspiring doctors!
- Muhammad Ahmed (Senior-year Civil Engineering & Computer Science Student at KAIST), who provided a comprehensive overview of how to apply to prestigious universities worldwide in various majors. His advice on excelling in applications for top-tier institutions was incredibly inspiring!

We were joined by bright students from:
- FGSC MM
- FGSC GM
- Engro Grammar School
- Fatima Fertilizer School

It was a day filled with insightful discussions, valuable guidance, and opportunities for students to explore diverse career paths. It's always inspiring to see the potential in our future leaders and change-makers!

Thank you to everyone involved for making this event a success, and we look forward to continuing this journey of helping students across Pakistan unlock their true potential. Together, we can shape a brighter future!
